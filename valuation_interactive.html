Summary: Digital Assets Valuation Opportunity

  1. Proof of Reserves (PoR) Support: $50-150K per engagement, quarterly recurring
  2. Fair Value Advisory: $75-200K per audit, annual + ad-hoc

Why Now?
  • VARA requires independent valuation attestations
  • VASPs need fair value expertise for IFRS compliance
  • Crypto funds require NAV calculations
  • Limited competition (most valuation firms lack crypto expertise)
What We Need
  • 1-2 valuation specialists with crypto training
  • Access to pricing data vendors (CoinMetrics, Kaiko)
  • Collaboration with audit blockchain specialists
  • 4-6 weeks to build initial capabilities

Bottom Line

Low barrier to entry, high demand, recurring revenue model. We can leverage existing audit relationships and valuation methodology, with minimal incremental investment to add digital asset pricing capabilities.

Revenue Drivers (Ranked by Near-Term Potential)

1. Proof of Reserves Support Quick Win

Service Description

Provide pricing and valuation support for auditor's Proof of Reserves attestations. Auditors verify on-chain balances; we provide fair value pricing at cutoff date.

Revenue Model

  • Pricing: $50-150K per PoR engagement (depending on number of assets and complexity)
  • Frequency: Quarterly or annual (many VASPs publish quarterly PoR)
  • Scope: Pricing data validation, principal market analysis, Level 1/2 classification

Why It's a Quick Win

  • Direct collaboration with our audit teams (built-in referral source)
  • Straightforward scope (pricing only, no complex modeling)
  • Recurring revenue (quarterly PoR cycles)
  • Can be delivered remotely (no on-site requirements)
12-Month Revenue Potential: $500K-1.5M (assuming 10-15 PoR engagements at $50-100K each)
2. Fair Value Advisory for IFRS Audits Quick Win

Service Description

Support audit teams with fair value measurement for digital assets held by VASPs, corporates, and funds. Includes pricing hierarchy assessment, valuation methodology review, and independent price testing.

Revenue Model

  • Pricing: $75-200K per audit (depending on portfolio size and complexity)
  • Frequency: Annual (tied to audit cycle), plus ad-hoc for interim reporting
  • Scope: Principal market determination, pricing source evaluation, NAV calculation review

Value Proposition

  • Audit independence maintained (valuation specialist is separate from audit team)
  • Accelerates audit completion (removes pricing bottleneck)
  • Reduces audit risk (independent valuation support)
12-Month Revenue Potential: $750K-2M (assuming 10-15 audit support engagements)
3. Portfolio Valuation for Crypto Funds High Revenue

Service Description

Monthly or quarterly NAV calculations for crypto hedge funds, venture funds, and asset managers. Includes pricing for liquid assets, valuation models for illiquid tokens, and administrator support.

Revenue Model

  • Pricing: $100-300K annually for monthly NAV (based on AUM and complexity)
  • Frequency: Monthly or quarterly recurring
  • Scope: Liquid crypto pricing, illiquid token valuation (Level 2/3), DeFi position valuation

Key Differentiators

  • Brand credibility (Deloitte name critical for institutional LPs)
  • Integration with fund audit (seamless handoff to audit team)
  • Ability to value complex instruments (options, derivatives, DeFi positions)
12-Month Revenue Potential: $500K-1.5M (assuming 5-10 fund clients at $100-150K each)
4. Purchase Price Allocation (M&A) Strategic

Service Description

Valuation of digital assets, customer relationships, and technology for M&A transactions involving crypto companies (exchanges, wallets, protocols).

Revenue Model

  • Pricing: $200-500K+ per transaction (based on deal size)
  • Frequency: Project-based (lumpy revenue)
  • Scope: Crypto asset valuation, intangible asset identification, goodwill allocation
Note: Requires more specialized expertise (DCF modeling, intangible asset valuation). Consider as Year 2 expansion after building core capabilities.
5. Tokenization Valuation Emerging

Service Description

Valuation for real-world asset tokenization (real estate, commodities, art). Includes underlying asset appraisal validation and token pricing framework.

Revenue Model

  • Pricing: $50-200K per tokenization project
  • Frequency: Project-based (growing market)
  • Scope: Underlying asset valuation, token economics review, pricing mechanism validation
Opportunity: Dubai is a hub for tokenization initiatives (DIFC, DMCC). High growth potential but requires cross-practice collaboration (real estate, art, commodities valuation).
